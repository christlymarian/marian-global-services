Overview: Large Print Adaptation & Accessibility (WCAG/508)

Large Print Adaptation is a compliance-critical service, particularly in the pharmaceutical, government, and finance sectors. It involves redesigning documentation to meet specific visual accessibility requirements (Section 508, WCAG).

This includes adjusting font size, line spacing, contrast, and layout complexity, ensuring the final localized documents are fully readable and compliant for users with visual impairments..

For enterprise clients, consistent quality is non-negotiable for brand trust and regulatory compliance. We manage your content's journey from file inception to final delivery, providing a secure, end-to-end solution.
